A first research experience in mathematics

Explore, collaborate, and discover mathematics.

SpinM is an intensive one-week summer program that brings together undergraduate students for a collaborative first research experience in mathematics.

Participants will investigate mathematical ideas, formulate conjectures, and work in small groups guided by faculty and graduate-student mentors.

Register to SpinM Explore the program


About the program

The program is designed to immerse undergraduate students in advanced mathematical topics not typically covered in the undergraduate curriculum through an inquiry-based format that encourages active exploration and collaboration.

The program centers on two broad mathematical themes—analysis and enumerative combinatorics—designed to inspire curiosity, mathematical creativity, and collaborative problem solving.
