Location

All activities of the program will be held on St Louis University North Campus in Ritter Hall 323.

The page below gathers practical information for participants: location, internet access, food options, and parking.

Internet Access

To access the internet on the SLU campus, follow the instructions below:

  • Step 1: Turn on your Wifi and connect to SLUGuest.
  • Step 2: Click on “Accept” on the authorization request form. You should now be connected to the internet.

Parking Services

Campus visitors have two parking options:

  • Park in metered spaces and pay the meters ($2.25/hr). The closest street parking (metered) is on Lindell Blvd, East of Grand.
  • Visitor parking on campus ($2/hour or $6/day). If visitor parking is used on campus, the nearest locations are the Laclede or Olive/Compton garages.

*Note that the Thursday evening session of the MCMC will be held in the Allied Health Professional Building, which is near the Hickory East Parking Garage on the South Campus of Saint Louis University.
